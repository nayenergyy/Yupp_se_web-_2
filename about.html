About Advocate Rizwan Ahmed

Dedicated Legal Professional Serving Umaria Since 2012

Your Trusted Legal Partner

With over 12 years of dedicated practice in Umaria, Madhya Pradesh, I have built a reputation for excellence, integrity, and unwavering commitment to my clients' rights.

My journey began after completing my LL.B. from the prestigious National Law University, followed by specialized training in criminal and civil litigation. Since establishing my practice in Umaria in 2012, I have successfully represented clients in hundreds of cases across district courts, sessions courts, and the High Court of Madhya Pradesh.

What sets me apart is my personalized approach to each case. I believe that every legal matter is unique and requires careful analysis, strategic planning, and compassionate representation. My office maintains strict confidentiality and ethical standards in every interaction.
