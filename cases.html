Criminal Cases

Expert defense in serious criminal matters

Bail Applications

Anticipatory bail, regular bail, and special bail applications in criminal cases. Proven success rate in securing bail even in serious offenses.

Common Scenarios:

  • FIR registered under Sections 302, 307, 326 IPC
  • Arrest in economic offenses
  • Non-bailable warrant situations
  • High Court bail applications

Domestic Violence

Representation in cases under Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005. Securing protection orders and maintenance.

Key Services:

  • Protection order applications
  • Maintenance claims
  • Residence rights enforcement
  • Compensation claims

FIR Quashing

Applications to quash FIRs under Section 482 CrPC when allegations are frivolous, vexatious, or without merit.

When Applicable:

  • No prima facie case established
  • Mala fide intentions of complainant
  • Settlement between parties
  • Abuse of process of law

Criminal Trial Defense

Complete representation throughout criminal trial proceedings from charge framing to final arguments and judgment.
